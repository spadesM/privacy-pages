Privacy Policy

GlyphDecode — Ancient Cipher Puzzle Game

Last updated: March 22, 2026

1. Overview

GlyphDecode ("we", "our", "the app") is a free-to-play mobile puzzle game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress (daily puzzle history, blind mode history)
  • High scores (Fragment Blitz)
  • Settings preferences (sound, haptics, tutorials seen)

This data is stored in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data for ad personalization, including:

  • Device advertising identifier (Ad ID)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address (for approximate location)
  • App usage data

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data from partner apps.

4. Children's Privacy

GlyphDecode is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not comply with COPPA or similar children's privacy regulations as it is not targeted at minors.

5. Data Storage & Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. No accounts are created and no data is transmitted to external servers by our app. Third-party ad services may transmit data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

  • Delete your data: Clear the app's data through your device settings, or uninstall the app.
  • Opt out of ad personalization: Adjust your device's ad settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).
  • Reset advertising ID: Available in your device's privacy settings.

7. No Account Required

GlyphDecode does not require account creation, login, or any form of registration. There is no user authentication system and no personal data is collected through the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
