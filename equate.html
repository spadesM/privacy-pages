Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 21, 2026

EquatePro ("we", "our", "the app") is a free math puzzle game developed as a mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

EquatePro does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account creation, login, or registration.

The app stores your game progress (daily puzzle history, high scores, settings) locally on your device only using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob — We display advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Google Play Services — Required for app distribution and updates.

3. Advertising

EquatePro displays banner ads on the main menu and interstitial ads between games. These ads are served by Google AdMob. You may see personalized ads based on your Google ad preferences, which you can manage in your device's Settings → Google → Ads.

4. Children's Privacy

EquatePro is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app contains third-party advertisements (Google AdMob).

5. Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by clearing the app's storage in your device settings or uninstalling the app.

6. Security

We value your trust. Since we do not collect or transmit personal data to our own servers, there is no personal data at risk. Third-party services (Google AdMob) handle their own data security.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
